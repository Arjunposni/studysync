Help Center

Welcome to the StudySync Help Center! Here you can find answers to common questions and learn how to make the most of our platform.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and fill out the required information.

2. How can I reset my password?

If you forget your password, click on the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page to receive a password reset email.

3. How do I customize my study preferences?

You can customize your study preferences by navigating to the "Settings" dropdown menu and selecting "Study Preferences."

4. Can I sync my calendar with StudySync?

Yes! You can sync your Google Calendar or Outlook calendar by going to the "Sync & Integrations" section in the settings.
